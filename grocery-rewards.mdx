# Offer Management System (OMS) Documentation

## CVP Offer

# Grocery Rewards

This document explains how to configure the Grocery Rewards CVP offer in the Offer Management System (OMS).

---

## 1. Label

**(Categorize offers by travel, food, beauty)**

- Create a new label or choose an existing label from the dropdown.

---

## 2. Banner Title

**(Banner title to be shown to the customer in PWA)**

The Banner Title is displayed on the PWA Home Page as the main heading of the offer banner. It is the first thing customers see while browsing available offers and is designed to quickly capture their attention.

![Banner Title 1](images/Banner Title 1.png)

### Banner Title Value

**Earn Rewards on Grocery Shopping**

![Banner Title 2](images/Banner Title 2.png)

---

## 3. Banner Description

**(Banner description to be shown to the customer in PWA)**

The Banner Description is displayed below the Banner Title on the PWA Home Page. It gives customers a short overview of the offer and encourages them to click and learn more.

![Banner Description](images/Banner Description 2.png)

### Banner Description Value

**Earn 2 Reward Points for every ₹100 spent on eligible grocery purchases.**

---

## 4. Display Title

**(Title of the Offer to be shown to the customer in PWA)**

The Display Title is shown on the Card Features page after the customer selects the offer from the PWA Home Page. It serves as the main heading for the offer and helps customers identify the benefit before viewing the complete offer details.

![Display Title 1](images/Display Title 1.png)

### Display Title Value

**Grocery Rewards**

![Display Title 2](images/Display Title 2.png)

---

## 5. Display Description

**(Description of the Offer to be shown to the customer in PWA)**

The Display Description is displayed on the Offer Details page below the Display Title. It provides customers with a detailed explanation of the offer, including the key benefits and rewards.

![Display Description 1](images/Display dis.png)

### Display Description Value

**Shop at eligible grocery stores and earn 2 Reward Points for every ₹100 spent.**

![Display Description 2](images/display dis 2.png)

---

## 6. Display Order

**(Prioritize display order)**

The Display Order determines the priority in which offers are displayed to customers. Offers with a lower display order appear before offers with a higher display order.

## 9. How It Works

1. Shop at eligible grocery stores using your eligible card.
2. Spend on qualifying grocery purchases.
3. Earn **2 Reward Points** for every ₹100 spent on eligible transactions.
4. Reward Points will be credited to your rewards account according to the rewards program timeline.
5. Redeem your accumulated Reward Points through the rewards program.

---

## 10. Terms and Conditions

- Valid on eligible grocery purchases only.
- Earn 2 Reward Points for every ₹100 spent.
- Cancelled or refunded transactions are not eligible.
- Standard terms and conditions apply.
