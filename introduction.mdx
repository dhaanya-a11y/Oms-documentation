# Offer Management System (OMS)

Welcome to the **Offer Management System (OMS)** documentation.

The Offer Management System (OMS) is used to create, configure, and manage different types of customer offers. It enables administrators to define offer details, configure display settings, upload images, and manage the customer experience in the Progressive Web App (PWA).

This documentation provides a step-by-step guide for configuring different offer types available in OMS.

---

## Offer Types Covered

### Customer Value Proposition (CVP) Offers

CVP Offers are designed to reward customers for eligible transactions. These offers help increase customer engagement by providing reward points, cashback, or other benefits.

**Example:**
- Grocery Rewards

---

### Merchant Offers

Merchant Offers provide discounts or benefits at specific partner merchants. Customers receive the offer when they make eligible purchases using their card.

**Example:**
- Domino's Pizza Offer
